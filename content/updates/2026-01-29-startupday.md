I participated in a panel discussion at sTARTUp Day 2026 in Tartu, Estonia.

The panel was titled **"Cutting Through the Hype – Quantum and AI Technology Limits, Lessons, and Next Moves"**. Together with Johannes Heinsoo (Senior Quantum Engineer at IQM) and Roland Matt (Senior Quantum Architect at Oxford Ionics), and moderated by Kalev Kaarna (Business Developer at Metrosert), we discussed the current realities, hardware/software limitations, and future outlook of quantum computing and artificial intelligence.

Read more about the panel and event resources on the [Kvant website](https://kvant.ee/en/quantum-technology-estonia/useful-resources/startup-day-2026/).
