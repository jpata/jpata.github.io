I had the opportunity to present the Center of Excellence - Fundamental Universe at the TeadusEST 2025 forum in Tartu, organized by ETAG - Estonian Research Council.

The Center of Excellence unites researchers from University of Tartu, TalTech – Tallinn University of Technology and NICPB / Keemilise ja Bioloogilise Füüsika Instituut (KBFI) working on fundamental physics, including on CERN and ESA projects.

Our team members contribute to measurements of the Higgs boson at unprecedented precision, develop novel ML-based software and algorithms for data reconstruction, develop ultrafast electronics for triggering, and much more!

What did we accomplish? What lies ahead?
